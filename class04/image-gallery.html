how to use image lightbox

  • download jquery and lightbox
  • add lightbox.min.css, jquery-3.7.1.min.js and lightbox.min.js in your page
  • add data-lightbox="mygallery" in your image anchor links like this:
    <a href="../assets/images/DSC_0001.jpg" data-lightbox="mygallery"> <img src="../assets/images/DSC_0001.jpg" alt="" class="img-fluid" width="200px"> </a>
  • your gallery will be grouped based on data-lightbox attribute value
